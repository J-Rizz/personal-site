Helfy

Helfy allows tenants of Helvetia Real Estate to easily request repairs and handle administrative issues online. It automates issue resolution processes for the Helvetia Real Estate team, decreasing response times and allowing Helvetia to build a better relationship with their tenants.

I owned the entire design process for Helfy. From user discovery and process mapping, through prototyping, usability testing and product specs, all the way to feedback collection, analysis and managing continuous product improvements.

Network 68

Network 68 is an accelerator program that helps founders get from an idea to raising seed funding for their project through a series of hands-on workshops and coaching sessions. We also provide ad-hoc presentations or workshops on how to start a business, build products, pitch for and raise funding and more.

As a founding partner, I created Network 68’s visual identity, design guidelines and promotional materials. EventHunt

EventHunt is an aspiring go-to place for finding out which start-up events are worth attending. Thanks to reviews by event attendees, founders know whether to expect good networking opportunities, top keynote speakers or VCs looking to invest without taking the risk and blowing their bootstrapped budget on expensive tickets for nothing.

As a side project between 2 founders, EventHunt is my playground for branding, UX, UI design and lean product management methodologies all in one. Thanks to implementing the project through Bubble.is, we can keep the release cycle short and build - measure - learn to out hearts’ content.

PapayaPods

PapayaPods was a rental housing management platform, helping landlords with finding new tenants for their vacant properties. Once a tenant moved in, landlords could collect rent payments online, receive repair requests, save important documents and communicate with their tenants online.

Throughout the project, I led PapayaPods as the product owner. I worked closely with James Rae (graphic design), Mark Diffey (UX design) and the dev team to effectively manage the product backlog, weekly sprints and the design process in line with the company objectives.
