What I do and what I enjoy.

Work.

Process optimisation

With experience in consulting and building web apps for automating operations in both B2C and B2B setting, I can map out existing processes, figure out what drives them and how to bring them online.

Product specs

I don’t flinch at nitty-gritty spec work. Human-readable specs in Gherkin, UML flowcharts, user stories and Q&A sessions help me make sure everyone’s on the same page.

UX/UI design

Although not a UX nor UI designer by trade, I have a keen eye for design. I work closely with designers and can step in and fill the role if need be. I use Adobe Xd and InVision to create, test and iterate on prototypes.

Testing & analysis

I have experience using HotJar, Google Analytics and conducting user testing sessions to formulate and validate hypotheses. I can also bend excel to my will and work with internal logs on user behaviour.

Backlog management

I use ProdPad to keep track of all aspects of the product. With my finger on the pulse of the business and regular Planning Poker sessions with the devs, I make sure the right things get built on time.

Web development

I’m not a web developer by trade, but I speak “the language” and know enough HTML and CSS by now to lend a helping hand if needed or create a simple static prototype.
