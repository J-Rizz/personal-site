This is my story.

Where I come from and what shaped me.

01/ Exploring the World

Thanks to my dad's talent and hard work, I was lucky enough to travel and see the world as a kid. Seeing life beyong the borders of Slovakia awoken my desire and determination to learn, get out there and achieve something bigger than myself.

02/ GBAS - Bilingual high school

GBAS is a truly special place. Studying in English was the ticket abroad I needed. More importantly though, the lifelong friends, inspiring teachers and a strong feeling of community I found there shaped my life ever since.

03/ EHL, Switzerland

EHL was an eye-opener. It was an introduction to the world of business, hard work and responsibility. It was one of the most intense and formative periods of my life.

04/ Co-founding a start-up

Initially working nights and weekends alongside a full time job, I set out on roller coaster of successes and failures with a team of wonderful people by my side. Pursuing this vision was a giant leap of faith and I am glad I had the guts to do it.

05/ Move to Barcelona

Leaving the comfort and familiarity of the life I’ve built up in Lausanne over 7 years was hard, but it was one of the best things I’ve ever done. Barcelona is pulsing with life, it is an inspiring and wonderful city of opportunity and endless inspiration.

06/ Becoming a PM

Co-founders of start-ups rarely have a fixed job description. They simply do whatever needs to get done. As it happens, I’ve gradually taken up most of what a dedicated PM would (and some on top of it) and I found my passion in this role.

07/ Learning to code

I’ve always been passionate about tech, got into UX/UI design as a PM and learned a great deal about web development on the job, but still felt like a piece of the puzzle was missing. So I’m now learning how to bring my ideas, designs and specs to life.

Short Bio

Studied hospitality and business at EHL, Switzerland. Co-founded an aspiring student housing company. Executed feasibility studies for new real estate developments. Pivoted towards software, started PapayaPods. Led the product from sketches to a working platform with 6’000+ users. Executed custom B2B software development projects as PM.
